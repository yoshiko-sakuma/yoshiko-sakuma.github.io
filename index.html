AT2030

Identifying the unmet needs of Assistive Technology in LMICs, the prevalence of functional disability and use/unmet needs of rehabilitation services with quant/qual data.
The SHINE project, a mixed method study: shining spotlight on the inclusion of disabled participants in clinical trials

Collected primary data (quant/qual) from the people living with disabilities, their families, and clinical trial staff to identify the potential obstacles people living with disability participating in clinical trials and how clinical trials could be more inclusive.Funded by the EDI Seed Fund
